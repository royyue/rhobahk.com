公司概览

Company Overview

“海外盛誉 · 崇尚完美 · 跨境枢纽”

东莞浩霸新能源科技有限公司是一家在进出口全球大宗贸易、新能源产业供应链领域深具实力的创新科技商贸企业。公司深度扎根于中国南方的制造业名城——东莞，依托粤港澳大湾区无与伦比的完整产业链条与成熟物流生态，深耕“锂电新能源、前沿电子、智能家居”三大核心战略板块，构建起多维、敏捷的商品流通中枢。

作为大湾区具备全面整合能力的供应链企业，我们不仅向下深扎东南亚及全球优质制造源头，更能向上对接国内庞大的买家网络与大型生产实体。我们紧密依托本土实业配套，发挥大湾区精工制造与全球分拨分销的组合优势，为全球合作伙伴提供更高效、更具市场竞争力的全链条跨境大宗商品交付方案。

"Global Reputation · Pursuit of Perfection · Cross-border Hub"

Dongguan Rhoba New Energy Technology Co., Ltd. is a high-capacity international trading and supply chain enterprise. Deeply rooted in Dongguan, a world-famous manufacturing hub, the company leverages the comprehensive industrial cluster of the Greater Bay Area. We focus on three strategic pillars: "Lithium Battery New Energy, Advanced Electronics, and Smart Home", creating a highly efficient international trade pipeline.

As a highly dynamic supply chain hub in Southern China, we connect top-tier overseas manufacturing sources with a broad industrial customer network. Backed by solid local industrial integration, we utilize GBA's precision manufacturing and flexible distribution networks to deliver highly competitive global commodity solutions and premium trade services.

Lithium Battery & New Energy Portfolio

立足东莞业务中枢 · 绿色动力供应链枢纽

锂电新能源是浩霸集团倾力打造的支柱核心。业务全面以东莞浩霸新能源科技有限公司为主导开展实施，深度对接东南亚等海外专业新能源制造厂商，构建起稳固的高品质动力电芯与系统模组海外采购链。公司在合规法制化框架下，以服务大中华区市场的进口大宗贸易为主体，同时协同海外其他区域的出口贸易。

依托大湾区卓越的物流配套与技术落地承接能力，我们引入的优质新能源动力产品精准定向服务于国内及全球的新能源汽车制造大厂、工业级储能设备商、机器人制造巨头以及高功率电动工具企业，提供安全、稳定、具备强竞争力的跨境绿色动力核心组件供应链方案。

Anchored by Dongguan Core · Green Power Supply Chain Hub

The lithium new energy sector stands as the group's vital pillar, with operations led primarily by our Dongguan Rhoba New Energy Tech company. We deeply engage professional new energy manufacturers in SE Asia, establishing secure overseas procurement routes for high-quality power cells and system modules. Our framework heavily focuses on compliant import trade serving the Greater China market, paired with supplementary exports globally.

Leveraging the Greater Bay Area's excellent logistics support and technology implementation capabilities, our premium battery products precisely serve major EV automakers, industrial energy storage manufacturers, robotics pioneers, and high-power electric tool enterprises worldwide, delivering secure and highly stable green power core component supply chain solutions.
