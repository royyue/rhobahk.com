集团概览

Group Overview

“海外盛誉 · 崇尚完美 · 跨境枢纽”

浩霸集团（香港）控股有限公司成立于2015年，是一家在进出口对外贸易领域享有盛誉的跨国型综合企业。公司以香港自由港的国际化政策为资本引擎与战略孵化窗口，同时扎根中国南方制造业名城——东莞，深耕核心业务运营与产业链深度整合。我们致力于发挥两地协同效应，全面释放粤港澳大湾区的产业与区位潜力。

作为集团业务的核心引擎，东莞浩霸新能源科技有限公司全面协同大湾区完备的产业集聚实力，围绕“锂电新能源、前沿电子、智能家居”三大核心战略板块，构建起立体系、敏捷性的双向跨境供应链。我们向下深扎东南亚及全球优质制造源头，向上对接大中华区及海外庞大的买家网络，以东莞为核心枢纽，全力输出高效率的商品流通方案与高标准的跨境商贸服务。

"Global Reputation · Pursuit of Perfection · Cross-border Hub"

Rhoba Group (Hong Kong) Holding Company Limited, established in 2015, is a renowned multinational enterprise in global trade. The company leverages Hong Kong's international free port policies as a capital engine and strategic incubation window, while simultaneously establishing itself in Dongguan, a renowned manufacturing city in southern China, deeply cultivating core business operations and integrating the industrial chain. We are committed to maximizing the synergistic effects of both locations and fully unleashing the industrial and locational potential of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area.

As the primary engine of the group, Dongguan Rhoba New Energy Technology Co., Ltd. fully utilizes the robust industrial aggregation of the GBA. Focusing on "Lithium Battery & New Energy, Advanced Electronics, and Smart Home", it establishes an agile, three-dimensional two-way cross-border supply chain. Anchored by the Dongguan operation hub, we connect with global manufacturing sources and extensive buyer networks to deliver efficient commodity circulation and premium trade services.

锂电池新能源

Lithium Battery & New Energy

立足东莞业务中枢 · 绿色动力供应链枢纽

锂电新能源是集团倾力打造的支柱核心。业务全面以东莞浩霸新能源科技有限公司为主导开展实施，深度对接东南亚等海外专业新能源制造厂商，构建起稳固的高品质动力电芯与系统模组海外采购链。公司在合规法制化框架下，以服务大中华区市场的进口大宗贸易为主体，同时协同海外其他区域的出口贸易。

依托大湾区卓越的物流配套与技术落地承接能力，我们引入的优质新能源动力产品精准定向服务于国内及全球的新能源汽车制造大厂、工业级储能设备商、机器人制造巨头以及高功率电动工具企业，提供安全、稳定、具备强竞争力的跨境绿色动力核心组件供应链方案。

Anchored by Dongguan Core · Green Power Supply Chain Hub

The lithium new energy sector stands as the group's vital pillar, with operations led primarily by our Dongguan Rhoba New Energy Tech company. We deeply engage professional new energy manufacturers in SE Asia, establishing secure overseas procurement routes for high-quality power cells and system modules. Our framework heavily focuses on compliant import trade serving the Greater China market, paired with supplementary exports globally.

Leveraging the Greater Bay Area's excellent logistics support and technology implementation capabilities, our premium battery products precisely serve major EV automakers, industrial energy storage manufacturers, robotics pioneers, and high-power electric tool enterprises worldwide, delivering secure and highly stable green power core component supply chain solutions.

前沿电子

Advanced Electronics

根植大湾区信息产业腹地 · 涵盖双向进出口业务

全面涵盖进口与出口双向业务。浩霸集团紧跟全球数智科技发展前沿，前沿电子板块核心深度依托于“世界工厂”东莞成熟完善的电子信息部件产业链。在出口贸易端，我们将高品质智慧终端、数码周边及消费电子高效推向全球海外市场；在进口贸易端，则依托东莞强大的制造消化能力，积极对接全球顶尖芯片与高端元器件渠道，引入高精尖工业级电子组件，在国际电子供应链条中扮演着灵活动态的商品流通枢纽。

Rooted in GBA IT Cluster · Covering Two-way Import & Export

The electronics sector comprehensively encompasses both import and export dimensions.
